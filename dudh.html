About Dudhsagar waterfalls

Dudhsagar, meaning “Sea of Milk,” is one of India’s tallest waterfalls, cascading majestically from a height of 310 meters. Surrounded by the lush greenery of the Western Ghats, it’s a spectacular sight, especially during the monsoon, when the falls roar with full force.
