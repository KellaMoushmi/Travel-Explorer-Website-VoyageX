About lepakshi

Lepakshi, located in Anantapur district of Andhra Pradesh, is famous for its rich heritage and Vijayanagara-style temple architecture. The Veerabhadra Temple, with its hanging pillar, intricate frescoes, and the massive monolithic Nandi statue, draws visitors from all over India

Specialty

Hanging pillar of Veerabhadra Temple Largest monolithic Nandi in India Exquisite Vijayanagara murals and carvings
