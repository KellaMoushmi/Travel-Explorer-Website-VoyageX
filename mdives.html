About Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, made up of over 1,000 coral islands surrounded by turquoise waters. Known for its luxury overwater villas, white sandy beaches, and vibrant marine life, it is one of the most sought-after honeymoon and holiday destinations in the world. With colorful coral reefs, crystal-clear lagoons, and breathtaking sunsets, the Maldives offers a perfect escape for relaxation and adventure
