About vijayawada

Vijayawada, often called the "Business Capital of Andhra Pradesh," is a vibrant city on the banks of the Krishna River. It is known for the Kanaka Durga Temple, Prakasam Barrage, and bustling markets. The city is also a major hub for education and commerce in the state.

Specialty

Kanaka Durga Temple on Indrakeeladri Hill Scenic Krishna River and Prakasam Barrage Rich blend of spirituality and modern lifestyle
