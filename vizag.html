About vizag

Visakhapatnam, popularly known as Vizag, is a vibrant coastal city in Andhra Pradesh located along the Bay of Bengal. Famous for its pristine beaches, scenic hills, and cultural heritage, Vizag beautifully blends natural beauty with modern infrastructure. It’s also called the “Jewel of the East Coast.”

Specialty

Home to RK Beach and Rushikonda Beach, perfect for sunrise and water sports. India’s Eastern Naval Command Headquarters, showcasing maritime importance. Rich heritage with Kailasagiri Hills, Simhachalam Temple, and museums
